About {{ site.data.event.name }}

{{ site.data.event.short_name }} is a yearly regional Python conference held in the late winter in Nashville, TN. {{ site.data.event.name }} will be our 7th year, and like every year before it, promises to be our best yet! There's a bit of something for everyone at {{ site.data.event.name }}, and if you'd like to know more about it, please visit our About page.

{{ site.data.event.short_name }} is a nonprofit event facilitated by TechFed Nashville, a Tennessee nonprofit organization whose mission is to support and grow the grassroots tech talent in Middle Tennessee through educational events and groups. TechFed provides financial stewardship, risk management and volunteer leadership development for Nashville-area technology events.
